HOBBIES

Gaming

Gaming has always been one of my biggest passions. I enjoy both competitive and story-driven games, often diving deep into immersive worlds and complex strategies. It's more than just entertainment — it's how I unwind and sometimes get inspired.

Music

I listen to music throughout the day — from chill beats while working to synth-heavy tracks that energize me. Genres like synthwave, lo-fi, and ambient keep me grounded and focused. It's the rhythm behind my daily flow.

Riding

There's something freeing about riding my motorcycle through open roads and unfamiliar places. It clears my mind, helps me reflect, and gives me a sense of calm. Whether it's a short city ride or a weekend escape, it's always worth it.

Exploring

I love discovering new places — from hidden coffee shops to distant provinces I've never seen before. Exploring helps me reset and sparks creativity by changing my perspective. It's an adventure I seek whenever I have time to spare.
